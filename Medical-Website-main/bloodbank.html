Blood Bank

Advanced Blood Bank Services

At Swasthya Point, we provide comprehensive blood bank services to ensure safe and timely access to blood products for patients in need. Our state-of-the-art facility adheres to the highest standards of quality and safety, enabling us to meet the transfusion needs of our community.

Our Blood Bank Services

We offer a full range of blood bank services to support patient care:

  • 1. Blood Donation

    We organize regular blood donation drives and provide facilities for voluntary blood donations to ensure a steady supply of blood for those in need.

    Donation Types: Whole blood, platelets, and plasma donations.

  • 2. Blood Collection and Testing

    Our trained staff collects blood samples and conducts thorough testing to ensure the safety and compatibility of blood products before transfusion.

    Testing Includes: Infectious disease screening, blood typing, and cross-matching.

  • 3. Component Separation

    We utilize advanced technology to separate blood into its components—such as red blood cells, plasma, and platelets—allowing for more targeted treatment options for patients.

    Benefits: Efficient use of blood products and tailored therapies for various medical conditions.

  • 4. Blood Transfusion Services

    Our blood transfusion services ensure that patients receive safe and appropriate blood products based on their medical requirements.

    Protocols: Strict adherence to transfusion guidelines and monitoring of patients during transfusion.

  • 5. Blood Storage and Preservation

    We maintain optimal storage conditions for blood products to ensure their viability and safety until they are needed for transfusion.

    Storage Facilities: Controlled temperature and environment for maximum preservation.

  • 6. Patient Support and Education

    We provide educational resources and support for patients and their families regarding blood donation, transfusion processes, and safety measures.

    Topics Covered: Importance of blood donation, transfusion reactions, and recovery.

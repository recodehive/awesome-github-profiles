Code of  Conduct

1

Do not spam.

If any member is found spamming, they will face consequences. Spamming includes excessive copy-pastes, meme/links/GIF spam, phishing links, NSFW content, and more.

2

No harassment or insults.

Harassment, insults, threats, gender-based insults, and cyberbullying will not be tolerated. Organizers will take strict action on any reported incidents.

3

Respect others' privacy.

Respect personal boundaries. Sharing sensitive information without consent or invading privacy will result in immediate action by organizers.
